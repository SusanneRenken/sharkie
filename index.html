Welcome to Shark Adventure!

Guide a small shark as it swims through the vast ocean. Collect coins to increase your score and gather poison to strengthen your special attacks. Be careful of the barriers and enemies that lurk in your path!

You'll face dangerous jellyfish and pufferfish along the way. Jellyfish can only be defeated using the Bubble Attack, while pufferfish require the powerful Fin Attack to be overcome. Make sure to use the right strategy for each enemy!

At the end of your journey, the mighty Endboss awaits. It won't be easy to take it down - only poisoned bubbles or the Fin Attack can harm it. Be brave and use your skills wisely to emerge victorious!

Good luck, and happy swimming!
